About Saikrishna Construction

Established in 2008, SaiKrishna Construction is a trusted civil contracting firm based in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, Maharashtra. Founded by Mr. Sunil G. Bilapatte, who brought over 18 years of project management experience, the company has grown from a small team to a dedicated workforce of over 25 professionals.

We specialize in civil works with a strong focus on building and bridge construction. With successful project delivery across Marathwada, Solapur, Yavatmal, Amravati, and beyond, we cater to both private and government sectors, offering quality-driven, client-focused solutions built on trust and integrity.
